Winter Weather Advisory in Big Belt, Bridger and Castle Mountains

Winter Weather Advisory issued May 2 at 2:17PM MDT until May 2 at 6:00PM MDT by NWS Great Falls MT

  • Event Start: 2024-05-02T14:17:00-06:00
  • Event End: 2024-05-02T18:00:00-06:00
  • Event Certainty: Likely
  • Event Severity: Moderate
Event Description

  • WHAT: Snow. Additional snow accumulations up to two inches. Winds gusting as high as 35 mph.
  • WHERE: Big Belt, Bridger and Castle Mountains and Snowy and Judith Mountains.
  • WHEN: Until 6 PM MDT this evening.
  • IMPACTS: Travel could be difficult. Areas of blowing and drifting snow could reduce visibility.

Event Instructions

Slow down and use caution while traveling. The latest road conditions can be obtained by calling 5 1 1 or visiting the MDT Road Report website. To see a graphical representation of the degree of stress on young livestock please visit our webpage and select Local Programs then Cold Air Advisory for Newborn Livestock.
