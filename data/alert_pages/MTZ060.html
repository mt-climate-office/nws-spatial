Lake Wind Advisory in Southwest Phillips

Lake Wind Advisory issued May 2 at 2:56AM MDT until May 2 at 8:00PM MDT by NWS Glasgow MT

  • Event Start: 2024-05-02T10:00:00-06:00
  • Event End: 2024-05-02T20:00:00-06:00
  • Event Certainty: Likely
  • Event Severity: Moderate
Event Description

  • WHAT: Northwest winds 15 to 25 mph with gusts up to 35 mph expected.
  • WHERE: Fort Peck Lake.
  • WHEN: From 10 AM this morning to 8 PM MDT this evening.
  • IMPACTS: Strong winds and rough waves on area lakes will create hazardous conditions for small craft.
  • ADDITIONAL DETAILS: Rain and snow showers are expected today and tonight with cold temperatures that will increase the risk of hypothermia.

Event Instructions

Boaters on Fort Peck Lake should use extra caution since strong winds and rough waves can overturn small craft.
